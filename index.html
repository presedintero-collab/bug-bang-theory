The conceptual sequence

Five thresholds from nothingness to meaning

The BUG BANG model follows a chain of emergence in which difference precedes structure, structure allows information, and information opens the path toward intelligence.

01

Genesis

The moment in which an undifferentiated state becomes capable of distinction.

02

First Difference

The smallest possible separation: this and not-this, inside and outside, one and another.

03

Information

Difference preserved, compared and organized into relations that can carry meaning.

04

Intelligence

The capacity to recognize relations, adapt, select and transform informational structure.

05

Regenesis

A return not to the same beginning, but to a new cycle enriched by what has been learned.
